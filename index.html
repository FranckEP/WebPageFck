Vallenato

The vallenato is a representative musical genre of Colombia. "Interpret the world through stories that mix realism and fantasy, in songs that are nostalgic, joyful, sarcastic and humorous” UNESCO definition about Vallenato.

THE 6 BEST COLOMBIAN VALLENATO SINGERS YOU SHOULD LISTEN TO

Diomedes Diaz

Known as the "Cacique de la Junta", Diomedes Díaz was one of the best Colombian vallenato singers of all time. and most successful of all time. He had a 40-year career with more than 200 hit songs, but, unfortunately, he is no longer in this world.

Listen his music on YouTube Music

Kaleth Morales

He was a Colombian vallenato singer and songwriter, best known as the leader of the "Nueva Ola" ("New Wave") movement in Vallenato, having released singles such as Vivo en el Limbo.

Listen his music on YouTube Music

Iván Villazón

With his long musical career and awards, this is a great exponent of Colombian vallenato. His album "La Compañía" (The Company) is the most successful of his career, as all the songs were big hits in Colombia.

Listen his music on YouTube Music

Silvestre Dangond

He is the maximum exponent of the so-called "Vallenato Nueva Ola" and debuted in 2002 with an album that had 3 hit singles. "Necesito verte", "Muñeca de porcelana" and "Quién me mandó".

Listen his music on YouTube Music

Los Inquietos del Vallenato

Los Inquietos Del Vallenato are a Colombian group with 25 years of artistic experience, recognized nationally and internationally for their great successes in the vallenato genre with romantic airs.

Listen his music on YouTube Music

Rafael Orozco

He was a Colombian singer of vallenato music. He was one of the major representatives of Colombian popular folk music and was lead singer and co-founder, alongside fellow accordionist Israel Romero, of the vallenato group Binomio de Oro de América, which was very popular in Colombia, Mexico and Venezuela.

Listen his music on YouTube Music
